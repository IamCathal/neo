FAQ

What is this project?

Steam Friends Graphing is a NUIG Computer Science and Information Technology final year project built to experiment with the principle that six degrees of seperation seperate any two given people in the world through social connections.

What stack does this project use?

Golang, Docker, RabbitMQ, Grafana, InfluxDB, Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana and MongoDB

How do I get a steamID64 for a Steam account?

Each Steam account has a ID associated with it thats used behind the scenes to uniquely identify a given account. The easiest way to get the associated steamID64 for a given account is through steamIDFinder
